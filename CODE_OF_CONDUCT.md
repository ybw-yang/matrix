# Community Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free community for
everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or role.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code with empathy and technical clarity.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good intent.
- Respect differing experience levels, languages, and viewpoints.
- Keep issue and pull-request discussions focused on the project.
- Protect private information and follow the security reporting process.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption,
publishing private information, and other conduct that makes participation
unsafe are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove inappropriate content and may warn,
temporarily restrict, or permanently ban participants whose behavior violates
this policy. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented, and
handled privately where possible.

To report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately through the
repository owner's established contact channel. Do not include sensitive
personal information in a public issue. Reports will be reviewed as promptly
and confidentially as practical.

This policy applies in project repositories, community channels, events, and
other spaces where someone represents the MATRiX project.
